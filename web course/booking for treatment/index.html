Booking for treatment

Patients seeking hospital treatment are required to follow the rules and regulations prescribed by the medical authority. All patients must carry valid identification documents and, where applicable, their medical history or referral slips. Emergency cases will be given priority, while general treatments will be provided according to appointment schedules. Patients are expected to maintain discipline within the hospital premises, respect visiting hours, and cooperate with doctors, nurses, and staff. Use of mobile phones should be restricted in treatment areas, and hygiene protocols must be strictly followed. Payment of fees, submission of insurance details, and collection of prescribed medicines should be done as per hospital procedures. Failure to follow these rules may result in delay or denial of services.
